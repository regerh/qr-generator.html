Frequently asked questions

Are QR codes generated here permanent?

Yes. QR codes are generated entirely in your browser and encode the data directly. They work as long as the destination (e.g. the URL) works — no expiry, no account needed.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. Everything runs client-side in JavaScript. Your text, WiFi passwords, and contact info never leave your device.

What size should I use?

300px is fine for digital use. Use 500px if you plan to print — more pixels means the code stays crisp when scaled up.

Can I change the colors?

Yes. Make sure there's enough contrast between foreground and background — low-contrast codes may not scan reliably.
